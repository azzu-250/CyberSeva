About Me

Welcome! I'm Muhammad Azam, a student building my first steps in Information Technology not with a simple website, but with something I hope matters. This cyber crime reporting portal started as a semester project, but it grew into something more meaningful.


Every day, we hear about online scams, harassment, and fraud. I wanted to use what I’m learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a clear, calm space where reporting feels accessible, not overwhelming. This is more than code to me. it’s a small attempt to make the digital world feel a little safer for someone, somewhere.


This project marks the beginning of my journey in IT, blending what I’ve learned in class with something I care about.

My Project

CyberSeva - Cyber Crime Reporting Portal

CyberSeva is my first semester individual project for the HNDIT program. This platform aims to provide Sri Lankan citizens with a secure and user-friendly way to report various types of cyber crimes.

The portal features a comprehensive system for reporting, tracking, and managing cyber crime complaints, along with educational resources to raise awareness about online safety. It demonstrates my commitment to using technology for social good and cybersecurity awareness.
